## Overview
App 2.17.0 adds administrative controls and improves performance in high-traffic areas of the product.

- Expanded access management tools for administrators.
- Faster loading for large datasets and dashboards.

- Exportable access review reports.

### Changed

- Corrected occasional pagination inconsistencies in reports.
